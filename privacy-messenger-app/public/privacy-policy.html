Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Our core principle: Privacy Messenger is designed so that we cannot read your messages. We use end-to-end encryption, collect minimal data, and never sell your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • A randomly generated Unique ID (e.g., PRIV-XXXXXXXX)
  • A hashed version of your passphrase (we never store or see the actual passphrase)
  • A device fingerprint hash (used solely to enforce one-account-per-device)
  • Optional display name (if you choose to set one)

Message Data:

  • All messages are end-to-end encrypted before leaving your device
  • We store only the encrypted ciphertext — we cannot read your messages
  • Message metadata (timestamps, delivery status) is stored to provide the service

What we do NOT collect:

  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Real names or identity documents
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Location data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers

2. How We Use Information

We use the minimal data we collect solely to:

  • Provide the messaging service
  • Deliver messages to intended recipients
  • Prevent duplicate account creation
  • Authenticate your identity when you log in
  • Send push notifications for new messages (if you opt in)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Period.

We may only disclose data if required by law, and even then, we can only provide encrypted message data that we cannot decrypt.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Messages with expiration times are automatically deleted when they expire
  • Deleted messages are permanently removed from our servers
  • If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days

5. Security

  • End-to-end encryption using ECDH key exchange and AES-256-GCM
  • Passphrases hashed with bcrypt (12 rounds)
  • TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • No plaintext message storage on servers

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account at any time
  • Delete individual messages
  • Block other users
  • Export your data

7. Children's Privacy

Privacy Messenger is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at: privacy@privacymessenger.app
